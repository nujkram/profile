FIREOUT 6

The FIRE OUT 6 App is an Android Mobile Application from the Bureau of Fire Protection Region 6 that will allow the people who have android smartphones to contact the nearest Fire Station in case of emergency, browse for fire-related information, and learn more about the Bureau of Fire Protection and its endearing campaign to ensure a fire-safe nation.

This app takes advantage of the smartphone’s capability of sending an SMS, making Calls, and connecting through the Internet when reporting an emergency. Users can choose between these three options and either one will get them the help they need.
